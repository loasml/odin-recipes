Chocolate Mousse

Description

Chocolate mousse is a soft prepared sweet or savory dish. It normally contains cream, egg (white and yolk), and sugar, and is often flavoured with chocolate, coffee, caramel, pur√©ed fruits, or various herbs and spices, such as mint or vanilla. In the case of some chocolate mousse, the chocolate is melted. Other chocolate mousse are egg-less and are prepared using gelatin as the setting agent.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 ounces dark chocolate (62% cacao is ideal)
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup chilled heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tiny pinch salt
